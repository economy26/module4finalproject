Upcoming Events

Tanabata Star Festival

Date: July 7, 2025

Celebrate the romantic legend of Orihime and Hikoboshi with vibrant streamers, traditional music, and wish-making under the stars. Join us for street food, yukata parades, and cultural performances that light up the night.

Nagaoka Fireworks Festival

Date: August 2–3, 2025

Enjoy one of Japan’s biggest firework displays in Niigata. Watch the sky burst with color in a breathtaking display choreographed to music. Great for families, couples, and all festival lovers!

Aomori Nebuta Festival

Date: August 2–7, 2025

Massive illuminated floats parade through Aomori’s streets, accompanied by powerful drumming and energetic haneto dancers. Visitors are welcome to rent a costume and join the parade!
